A Success Story: Christie’s Journey

For years, my career and my passion for competitive tennis were slowly being stripped away by a failing right elbow. My diagnosis was a laundry list of damage: lateral epicondylitis with a 20% partial-thickness interstitial tear, distal tricep tendinopathy and a thickened ulnar nerve that caused constant numbness in my hand and fingers.

It got so bad that I couldn't grip a racquet with any strength. Even simple tasks, like lifting a cup of coffee, were agonizing. My entire career both teaching and playing were looking grim. I had tried everything: PRP injections, physical therapy, and various alternative medicines. Nothing worked.

The Turning Point

After receiving just 1 injection of stem cell therapy from Flora, the recovery was nothing short of miraculous:

  • 2 Weeks Post-Injection: The numbness in my fingers and hand began to dissipate. Shortly after, it was completely gone.
  • The Months Following: I slowly returned to the gym and competitive tennis. The "crunching" sensation was gone, and I no longer had to hyperflex the joint just to get it to feel "in place."

The Ultimate Test: Within three months of the procedure, I went on an intensive three-day fishing trip in Panama, fishing 10+ hours a day. I successfully brought in a 400-pound Marlin with absolutely no pain, a feat that would have been physically impossible just months prior.
